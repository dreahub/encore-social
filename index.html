Share without the pressure.

Encore is a calm social space for friends only. No visible like counts, no follower flexing, and every post comes with clear tags so people know what they are opening.

Designed to feel calmer.

Encore is built around softer interaction online. Profiles are private by default, engagement metrics stay hidden, and users choose how they want to share through blogs, vlogs, or photo posts.
